About ReelYield

ReelYield is a comprehensive Android app built by and for serious fishermen. Whether you're chasing fluke in Narragansett Bay, tautog along the rocky shores, or black sea bass in deeper waters, ReelYield helps you record every catch with precise location, tide, weather, rig, and bait details — all stored securely on your device with no cloud upload required.

Track patterns over time, discover your most productive tides and moon phases, and build a personal fishing journal that reveals what really works for your local spots. No ads, no account needed — just pure fishing data in your pocket.

Key Features

ReelYield combines smart logging with real-time marine data to help you fish smarter:

  • Log Catches — Record species, length, weight, rig/jig, bait/color, GPS location, and photos right from the boat or shore.
  • Interactive Map — See every catch pinned on a map to spot productive areas and patterns over seasons.
  • Tides & Marine Weather — Pulls NOAA and NWS data for tides, wind, air/water temp, and forecasts tailored to your location.
  • Statistics & Insights — Automatic analysis of your best tides, moon phases, rigs, baits, times of day, and personal records.
  • Tackle Planning Tools — Plan your next trip with gear recommendations based on your logged success.

Perfect for weekend anglers and tournament fishermen alike who want data-driven decisions without complicated subscriptions.
