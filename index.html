About ReelYield

ReelYield is a comprehensive Android app designed for fishermen to track their catches, view detailed statistics, and optimize their fishing trips. Every catch is logged with GPS location, tide stage, water/air temperature, moon phase, rig/jig, bait, and photos — all stored privately on your device with no account or cloud required.

Key Features

  • Log Catches
  • Interactive Map
  • Tides & Marine Weather
  • Statistics & Insights
  • Tackle Planning Tools

Use real data from your own trips (like the fluke, tautog, black sea bass, and dogfish you've already logged) to see patterns in tides, moon phases, best times, and most effective baits/rigs.
