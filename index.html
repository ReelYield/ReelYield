About ReelYield

Developed by an avid Rhode Island fisherman, ReelYield helps you turn good days on the water into great ones. Unlike basic log apps, ReelYield combines detailed catch tracking with analytics, environmental data, and gear recommendations to help you understand why you're catching (or not catching) fish.

All your data stays private and local on your device — no cloud servers, no account required, no personal info collected.

Why Anglers Choose ReelYield

  • Smart Catch Logging: Record species, length, weight, rig/jig, bait/color, GPS location, tide stage, moon phase, pressure change, water/air temp, and more — with one-tap photo integration.
  • Powerful Analytics: See your most productive tides, moon phases, times of day, rigs, baits, and colors — based on your actual catches (e.g., "Waxing Gibbous + outgoing tide = hot fluke action").
  • Real-Time Environmental Data: Pull NOAA tides and National Weather Service marine forecasts for your location, so you fish smarter, not harder.
  • Interactive Catch Map: Visualize every logged catch on Google Maps to spot patterns and productive spots over time.
  • Privacy-Focused: Zero remote data collection — your logs, photos, and locations never leave your phone.
  • Tackle Planner: Build and save gear setups tailored to species, conditions, and your personal stats.

Whether you're chasing fluke off Narragansett, tautog around Bonnet Shores, or black sea bass in deeper water, ReelYield helps you refine your approach and increase your yield.
